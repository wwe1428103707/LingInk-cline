# LingInk 灵砚

LingInk 灵砚 is an AI academic writing assistant that works inside VS Code. It helps researchers, students, and academic writers move from scattered notes to structured, polished manuscripts while keeping every action visible and under your control.

## What LingInk Helps With

1. Plan research topics, thesis statements, outlines, and chapter structures.
2. Read manuscript files, notes, drafts, and reference material to build context before suggesting changes.
3. Polish paragraphs, abstracts, introductions, and reviewer responses in an academic style.
4. Check argument flow, evidence gaps, citation needs, and possible logical weaknesses.
5. Apply edits through reviewable diffs so you can accept, adjust, or reject every change.

## Academic Writing Workflows

### Research Planning

Start with a topic, rough idea, or assignment requirement. LingInk can help narrow the question, compare possible directions, propose paper structures, and turn a broad research goal into a manageable writing plan.

### Literature Review Support

Use files, folders, URLs, and MCP tools to provide papers, notes, or search results as context. LingInk can summarize literature, cluster themes, identify gaps, and draft review sections that preserve your intended argument.

### Manuscript Revision

LingInk can edit local files directly in VS Code and present proposed changes as diffs. Use it to improve section transitions, strengthen claims, rewrite awkward paragraphs, align terminology, or prepare a cleaner submission draft.

### Citation and Evidence Checks

Ask LingInk to inspect whether claims have enough support, where citations may be needed, and whether references are being used consistently. It can also work with connected citation tools or custom MCP servers when available.

### Reviewer Response Drafting

Paste reviewer comments or response notes and ask LingInk to help draft a point-by-point response. It can separate agreement, revision actions, rebuttal language, and manuscript change summaries.

## Bring Your Own Model

LingInk supports providers such as OpenRouter, Anthropic, OpenAI, Google Gemini, AWS Bedrock, Azure, GCP Vertex, Cerebras, Groq, OpenAI-compatible APIs, and local models through LM Studio or Ollama. You stay in control of model choice, API keys, and cost.

## Human-in-the-Loop Editing

LingInk may read files, propose edits, use tools, or run approved commands depending on your task and settings. Every sensitive action is reviewable, and checkpoints let you compare or restore previous manuscript states.

## Context Tools

**`@url`:** Add web pages, journal guidelines, documentation, or reference material as markdown context.

**`@file`:** Add a manuscript, notes file, bibliography export, or rubric.

**`@folder`:** Add a full paper project, notes collection, or reference pack.

**MCP:** Connect specialized tools such as literature search, citation managers, local scripts, databases, or institutional services.
