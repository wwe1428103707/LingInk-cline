# Full Visibility and Control

**LingInk operates transparently, showing the files it reads and every proposed change.**

Review suggested edits, citation changes, and manuscript diffs before they are applied. Checkpoints make it easy to compare versions or restore an earlier draft, so your paper stays under your control.
